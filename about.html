About Us

Who We Are?

  • UNICA4U is a platform passionately dedicated to preserving and celebrating the world’s diverse and unique crafts. Starting with India’s rich heritage, our vision is to expand globally, bringing exquisite handcrafted treasures to customers All Around the World. UNICA4U is gateway to India's rich heritage of crafts and artistry.
  • It brings you a curated collection of handcrafted treasures, perfect for gifting or personal indulgence. From Aurangabad's elegant Himroo shawls and Kolhapur's iconic Kolhapuri Chappals to the timeless charm of Bareilly's jhumkas and the luxurious Pashmina shawls of Kashmir UNICA4U offers the best of India's craftsmanship—all at your fingertips.
